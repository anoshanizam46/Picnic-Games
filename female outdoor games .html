CAPTURE THE FLAG

Capture the Flag is an outdoor team game where two teams compete to steal the other’s flag, usually hidden in their territory, and bring it back to their own side without being tagged. Players must strategize and work together.

Frisbee, also known as a flying disc, is a recreational sport where a disc is thrown between players, aiming to catch it without letting it touch the ground. It can be played casually or in competitive formats .

Baseball is a team sport played between two teams of nine players each. The game involves one team batting and the other fielding. The batter tries to hit a pitched ball and run to a series of four bases to score runs.

SOCCER

Soccer, also known as football in most countries, is a team sport where two teams of 11 players compete to score goals by getting a ball into the opposing team's net. Players primarily use their feet to control and pass the ball.

Badminton is a fast-paced racket sport played either indoors or outdoors, where players or teams hit a shuttlecock over a net. The objective is to score points by landing the shuttlecock in the opponent's side of the court.
