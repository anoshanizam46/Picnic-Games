PASS THE PARCEL

Pass the Parcel is a party game where players sit in a circle and pass a wrapped parcel around while music plays. When the music stops, the player holding the parcel unwraps a layer, with the game continuing until the final prize is revealed.

PILLOW FIGHT

A pillow fight is a playful game where players use pillows to strike each other, often while on a bed or soft surface. The objective is to "tag" opponents by hitting them with a pillow, typically in a friendly, non-violent manner.Soft hits, big laughs—let the pillow fight begin!

MUSICAL CHAIR

Musical Chairs is a game in which players walk around a circle of chairs while music plays. When the music stops, they must quickly find a seat. One chair is removed after each round, and the last player remaining wins.Race against the music.

HOT POTATO

The hot potato game is a fun activity where players pass a potato or similar object around while music plays. When the music stops, the player holding the potato is out, and the last person remaining wins.

TREASURE HUNT

The Treasure game is a scavenger hunt where players search for hidden items or clues that lead to a final prize. The game is often themed and involves teamwork or problem-solving to uncover the treasure.
