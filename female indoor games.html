CHARADES

Charades is a popular party game where players act out words or phrases without speaking, while others try to guess what they are. The game is usually played in teams, with each round featuring a different word or category.

PICTIONARY

Pictionary is a drawing and guessing game where players take turns sketching a word or phrase while their team tries to guess it. The goal is to guess as many words correctly as possible within a set time limit. It’s a fun, fast-paced game .

Truth or Dare is a classic party game where players take turns choosing between answering a question truthfully or completing a dare. The game is meant to be encouraging players to reveal secrets or take on silly tasks.

MOCKTAIL

The Mocktail Game is a fun, interactive activity where players create non-alcoholic drink combinations (mocktails) using a variety of ingredients. Players may compete to come up with the tastiest or most creative mocktail. Mix, taste, and crown the ultimate picnic bartender!

TWISTER

Twister is a physical party game where players place their hands and feet on colored circles on a large mat, following the directions given by a spinner. The goal is to avoid falling as the game gets more challenging and players have to stretch and twist into increasingly difficult positions.
